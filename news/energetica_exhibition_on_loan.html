Energe­tica Exhibition on Loan

By Emma Larson, Science Journalist

Excitement is in the air as the Kristiansand Science Museum eagerly anticipates the arrival of the acclaimed Energetica exhibition on loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam. This immersive exhibition promises to take visitors on a journey through the fascinating world of energy, showcasing the power of the elements and how we harness them to shape our world.

The Energetica exhibition features a series of captivating installations designed to engage and inspire audiences of all ages. From the moment visitors step foot inside the museum, they will be transported into a realm where the forces of nature come alive before their eyes.

One of the highlights of the exhibition is the innovative use of solar energy to power lighting installations, demonstrating the incredible potential of renewable energy sources. Visitors will have the opportunity to witness firsthand how sunlight is captured and converted into electricity, illuminating the path towards a more sustainable future.

But the exploration doesn't end there. Step onto "Wind Island" and discover the secrets behind wind power as turbines harness and control the natural force of the wind to generate electricity. Through interactive displays and informative exhibits, visitors will gain a deeper understanding of how wind energy is harnessed and its vital role in powering our communities.

"We are thrilled to bring the Energetica exhibition to the Kristiansand Science Museum," said Nicolai Andreassen Bye, Director of the museum. "This immersive experience offers a unique opportunity for visitors to learn about the importance of renewable energy and the innovative technologies that are shaping our energy landscape."

The exhibition is not only educational but also interactive, allowing visitors to engage with hands-on activities and experiments that explore the principles of energy conversion and sustainability. Whether it's building miniature wind turbines or experimenting with solar panels, there's something for everyone to enjoy and learn from.

"We believe that by inspiring curiosity and sparking interest in science, we can empower visitors to make informed choices about energy consumption and conservation," added Nicolai Andreassen Bye.

The Energetica exhibition will be on display at the Kristiansand Science Museum from 21.06.24 - 21.07.24, offering a unique opportunity for visitors to immerse themselves in the captivating world of energy. Admission is included with museum entry, making it accessible to all who wish to embark on this enlightening journey.
