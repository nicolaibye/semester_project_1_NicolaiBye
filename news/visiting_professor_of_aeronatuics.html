Visiting Profe­ssor of Aero­nautics

By Alex Johnson, Staff Writer

Prepare for an exciting journey through the skies as the Kristiansand Science Museum welcomes Prof. Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) for a special series of lectures on the wonders of aeronautics. Families and children alike are invited to embark on this thrilling adventure, exploring the past, present, and future of flight with one of the leading experts in the field.

Step into the world of aviation pioneers and daring innovators as Prof. Widnall shares captivating stories and insights into the history of flight. From the first attempts at human flight to the groundbreaking achievements of modern aircraft, discover how dreams of soaring through the skies became a reality.

But the adventure doesn't stop there! Join us as we look ahead to the future of aeronautics and explore the possibilities of space travel. From rocket ships to Mars rovers, learn about the incredible technologies that are shaping the way we explore the cosmos.

"We're thrilled to have Prof. Widnall share her passion for aeronautics with our visitors," said Nicolai Andreassen Bye, Director of the Kristiansand Science Museum. "Her lectures promise to inspire curiosity and spark imaginations, showing us that the sky is not the limit when it comes to dreaming big."

Each lecture will be filled with interactive demonstrations and hands-on activities designed to engage minds and foster a love for science. From building paper airplanes to experimenting with miniature rockets, there will be plenty of opportunities for everyone to get involved and have fun while learning.

"I can't wait to share the excitement of aeronautics with all who attend," said Prof. Widnall. "Together, we'll soar to new heights and discover the endless possibilities that await us in the world of flight."

The lectures will take place on 12th, 13th and 14th of June, at 10:00 and 14:00, in the auditorium of the Kristiansand Science Museum. Admission is free for museum members and just 70 nok for non-members. Spaces are limited, so be sure to reserve your tickets early to secure your spot on this incredible adventure!

Join us as we take flight with Prof. Sheila Widnall and explore the wonders of aeronautics. It's an adventure you won't want to miss!

For more information, please contact us!.
