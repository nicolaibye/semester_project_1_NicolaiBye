Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Photo by Mika Baumeister on Unsplash

Robotics Showcase

Enter a world of mechanical marvels at the Robotics Showcase. Witness the diversity of robots, from towering industrial machines to humanoid creations. Through captivating demonstrations and interactive displays, visitors explore the applications of robotics in fields like manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration. Engage with robots firsthand and discover the cutting-edge technologies shaping the future of automation.

DATE: 28th apr - 17th may

Photo by Markus Spiske on Unsplash

AI Interactive Lab

Step into the forefront of artificial intelligence at the AI Interactive Lab. Immerse yourself in hands-on experiments and demonstrations, uncovering the mysteries of machine learning, neural networks, and other AI techniques. Through interactive experiences, visitors gain insight into how AI is revolutionizing industries and reshaping the way we interact with technology.

DATE: 5th may - 20th june

Photo by Possessed Photography on Unsplash

Robotic Surgery Simulation

Experience the future of medicine in the Robotic Surgery Simulation. Step into a simulated operating theater and discover the intricacies of robotic surgery. Through virtual reality or interactive displays, participants learn about minimally invasive techniques and control robotic surgical systems. Explore how robotics is transforming healthcare and enhancing surgical precision for better patient outcomes.

DATE: 20th may - 30th june
