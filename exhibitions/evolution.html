Journey of Life

For centuries, philosophers and scientists have wrestled with the question of our origins. Where do we come from and how did we get here? Since Darwin proposed his theory of evolution we have had a framework for exploring and understanding our place in this world.
Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Timeline of Life

Embark on a journey through the ages with the Timeline of Life exhibit. Explore the evolutionary history of Earth, from its earliest inhabitants to the present day, through a dynamic and interactive timeline. Witness the emergence of diverse life forms, major evolutionary transitions, and the interconnectedness of all living organisms on our planet.

DATE: 28th apr - 17th may

Fossil Dig Site

Uncover the secrets of the past at the Fossil Dig Site. Step into the shoes of a paleontologist as you participate in a simulated excavation, uncovering replica fossils buried beneath the surface. Learn about the process of fossilization, the clues fossils provide about ancient life forms, and the fascinating stories they tell about evolution over millions of years.

DATE: 5th may - 20th june

DNA Discovery Lab

Delve into the building blocks of life at the DNA Discovery Lab. Engage in hands-on experiments to extract and analyze DNA samples from different organisms. Explore genetic similarities and differences to uncover evolutionary relationships and gain insights into the role of DNA in tracing ancestry and understanding the mechanisms of evolution.

DATE: 20th may - 30th june
