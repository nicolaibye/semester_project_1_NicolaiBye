Cosmic Adven­tures

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.
Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.

Planetarium Dome

Enter a mesmerizing simulation of the night sky inside the Planetarium Dome. Experience the beauty of constellations, phases of the moon, and cosmic phenomena with a state-of-the-art projector, offering an immersive journey through the wonders of the universe.

DATE: 28th apr - 17th may

Interactive Exoplanet Explorer

Embark on an interstellar journey with the Interactive Exoplanet Explorer. Explore alien worlds beyond our solar system through interactive displays, manipulating parameters to uncover their unique characteristics and ponder the possibility of life in distant cosmic realms.

DATE: 5th may - 20th june

Black Hole Simulator

Experience the captivating pull of a black hole in the Simulator. Through immersive visuals and interactive demonstrations, visitors witness how these cosmic phenomena warp space and time, gaining a deeper understanding of their mysterious nature and profound impact on the universe.

DATE: 20th may - 30th june
