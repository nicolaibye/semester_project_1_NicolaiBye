Marvels of Life

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world.

For many people their life expectancy is much longer and their quality of life much improved, thanks to the growth of our understanding of medicine. Over a series of exhibits we explore the history of medicine and take a look at some major breakthroughs including the discovery of penicillin and the first heart transplant.

Many of our great medicinal discoveries have come not only from the lab, but also from observing animals in the wild. Often our fellow creatures have beat us to it.

Cellular Microscopy Gallery

Step into the mesmerizing world of cellular biology at the Cellular Microscopy Gallery. Explore breathtaking images and videos captured through cutting-edge microscopy techniques, revealing the intricate structures and processes within cells. From the elegant dance of organelles to the dynamic interactions of molecules, visitors are immersed in the beauty and complexity of life at the microscopic level, gaining insights into the fundamental building blocks of living organisms.

DATE: 28th apr - 17th may

Human Anatomy Theater

Journey into the depths of the human body at the Human Anatomy Theater. Through immersive technology such as virtual reality or augmented reality, visitors embark on a captivating exploration of different body systems, organs, and tissues. From the beating heart to the intricate network of nerves, this interactive experience offers a deeper understanding of human anatomy, physiology, and the marvels of biological engineering that sustain life.

DATE: 5th may - 20th june

Photo by Drew Hays on Unsplash

Regenerative Medicine Pavilion

Discover cutting-edge medical innovations at the Regenerative Medicine Pavilion. Learn about regenerative therapies, including stem cell treatments and tissue engineering, and their potential to revolutionize healthcare. Explore the future of medicine and the possibilities of regenerating damaged tissues and organs.

DATE: 20th may - 30th june
